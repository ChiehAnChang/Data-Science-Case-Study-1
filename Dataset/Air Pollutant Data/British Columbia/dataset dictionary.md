## Dictionary: British Columbia Air Pollution (PM2.5) — Hourly Data

| Feature name | Definition |
|-------------|------------|
| Date | The date of the measurements in British Columbia time, in the format **MM/DD/YYYY**. |
| Time | The time of the measurements in British Columbia time, represented as integers from **1 to 24**. There is no **0**. The end of a day is represented as **24:00**. |
| PM25 | The PM2.5 concentration, measured in **µg/m³**. |
